424108_12VDC_4A power supply

EQF1283 VAC Switch Box
426081 VAC Harness
Custom 9ohm/200W Resistor Harness
Multimeter w/ Banana Jacks

Setup:
Connect one end of the EQF1283 to the 426081 harness.
Connect one end of the banana jacks to the custom resistor harness
Set multimeter to measure DC voltage


Test:
Verify ESD packaging.
Connect the custom resistor harness to the power supply.
Connect the 426081 harness to the power supply.
Switch on the EQF1283.
Verify that 12VDC is measured on the multimeter.
Switch off the EQF1283.
Disconnect the power supply and repeat test for the rest of the sample.
